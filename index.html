Privacy Policy for WordHunt

Effective Date: July 23, 2026

Last Updated: July 23, 2026

Velara Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the WordHunt mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use WordHunt.

By using WordHunt, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

WordHunt stores certain game-related information locally on your device to provide game functionality and save your progress.

This information may include:

  • Completed levels
  • Stars and scores
  • Coins and in-game currency
  • Hints and power-ups
  • Achievements
  • Daily challenge progress
  • Gallery and image collection progress
  • Game settings
  • Language preferences
  • Other gameplay preferences

WordHunt does not currently require user registration or user accounts.

Velara Games does not currently operate servers that store your personal game progress or gameplay data.

You can remove locally stored game data at any time by:

  • Using the Reset Progress option within the game, if available;
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings; or
  • Uninstalling the application.

2. Advertising

WordHunt uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner advertisements and rewarded advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising Identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information
  • App information
  • IP address
  • Diagnostic information
  • Ad interaction data
  • Performance and fraud-prevention information

This information may be used to:

  • Deliver advertisements
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Prevent fraud
  • Personalize ads where permitted by applicable law and device settings

For more information, review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies

3. Third-Party Services

WordHunt uses third-party services to provide certain functionality, including:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Google Fonts for fonts
  • Unsplash for game images

When content from these providers is loaded, they may receive technical information necessary to deliver that content, such as your IP address, device or browser information, and network-request information.

Third-party privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Unsplash Privacy Policy

4. In-App Purchases

WordHunt does not currently offer real in-app purchases.

If real in-app purchases are introduced in a future version of the game, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Data Retention

Game progress stored locally on your device remains available until you:

  • Reset your game progress;
  • Clear the application's data; or
  • Uninstall the application.

Information collected by third-party providers, including Google AdMob, Google, and Unsplash, is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

WordHunt is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by WordHunt, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the game, legal requirements, or third-party services.

When we make changes, we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
